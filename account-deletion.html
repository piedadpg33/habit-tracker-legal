Account Deletion in Habit Tracker

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their account

How to request account deletion?

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings or Profile section.
  3. Select the Delete Account option.
  4. Confirm the deletion of your account.
  5. If you encounter any issues, contact support at piedaddev@gmail.com.

What data is deleted?

  • Your profile and personal data associated with the account.
  • Habit history and records created by you.

What data is retained and for how long?

No personal data is retained after account deletion, except as required by law.

  • Data required to fulfill legal obligations (e.g., transaction records) is retained for up to 12 months.
  • Anonymous and aggregated data for statistical analysis may be retained indefinitely.
